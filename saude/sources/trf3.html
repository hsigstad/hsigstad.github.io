TRF3

Federal health cases from the 3rd Region covering São Paulo and Mato Grosso do Sul.

Overview

TRF3 (Tribunal Regional Federal da 3ª Região) is the federal regional court covering São Paulo and Mato Grosso do Sul. It handles cases where the federal government (União) is a defendant — typically demands for high-cost medications and treatments, particularly CEAF Group 1A drugs whose funding is a federal responsibility.

TRF3 health cases are structurally different from state-court (TJSP) cases: they tend to involve more expensive treatments and have different defendant profiles. Notably, the TRF3 data includes judge, vara, and comarca fields, making it potentially useful for extending the judge IV strategy to federal-jurisdiction cases.

Available columns: tribunal, text, number, date, judge, vara, comarca.
