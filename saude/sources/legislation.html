Health Legislation

Federal health legislation from Planalto and SaudeLegis.

Overview

The legislation corpus is compiled from two federal sources:

  • Planalto — the federal legislation portal (planalto.gov.br), containing laws, decrees, and other federal legal instruments
  • SaudeLegis — the Ministry of Health's legislation system (saudelegis.saude.gov.br), containing health-specific portarias, resoluções, and regulatory instruments

Key legislation for the project includes:

  • Lei 8.080/1990 — SUS organic law, establishing the Unified Health System
  • Lei 8.142/1990 — SUS funding and social participation mechanisms
  • Portaria 42/1999 — functional expenditure classification used by TCE-SP

Available columns: fonte, tipo, orgao, numero, data, ementa, termos_saude, url, texto_length.
